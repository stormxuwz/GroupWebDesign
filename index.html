Welcome

The EISA group is investigating improved methods for modeling complex environmental systems so that informed management-level decisions can be made under conditions of uncertainty. Coupling machine learning approaches with sensor data and advanced information technology, innovative and cost-effective solutions to complex environmental problems are being investigated.

Recent news:

New way to crowd-source design of green infrastructure

Development of a novel computational green infrastructure design framework that integrates interactive, neighborhood-scale, collaborative design by multiple stakeholders ("crowd-sourced design") with multi-scale models of ecosystem and human impacts.
